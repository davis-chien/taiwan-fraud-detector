# Fake Job Offer / Work-From-Home Scam

Category: 假求職詐騙

Description:
Scammers post fake job listings or send unsolicited messages offering easy, high-paying work-from-home jobs — typically "online task completion," "social media engagement," or "part-time e-commerce assistant." Early tasks are simple and pay small amounts to build trust. The victim is then asked to prepay a deposit or purchase products/gift cards as part of the "job," losing money when the scammer disappears.

Variants:
1. **Task-completion scam (刷單詐騙):** Victim is asked to "complete orders" on a fake e-commerce platform, prepaying for products that earn "commission." Early rounds pay out to build trust; later rounds require larger prepayments that are never returned.
2. **Data entry / part-time assistant:** Asks victim to click ads, follow accounts, or complete surveys for daily pay. Escalates to asking for prepayment of "system registration fee."
3. **Overseas work recruitment:** Promises high-salary jobs in Southeast Asia; victim is trafficked and forced to work in scam call centers.

Common signals:
- unsolicited job offer with no formal application: 「看到您的履歷，有個適合您的工作機會」
- high pay for minimal effort: 「一天只需2小時，月收入6萬以上」
- work from phone or computer, no experience required
- request to prepay or purchase before receiving pay: 「請先充值NT$2,000到工作帳戶」
- platform accessible only via a private link or LINE group
- job requires buying gift cards or making bank transfers

Example messages:
> 您好，我在104看到您的履歷，我們公司有兼職機會，在家工作每天只需30分鐘，日薪2000元，有興趣嗎？

> 我們正在招募LINE兼職人員，工作內容是幫商家按讚評論，每筆任務完成後即時結算，月收入3-5萬，請私訊了解詳情。

> 【海外高薪招募】泰國/柬埔寨客服專員，底薪8萬+提成，包吃住，簽證協助，有意者請聯繫：https://jobs-oversea.xyz
