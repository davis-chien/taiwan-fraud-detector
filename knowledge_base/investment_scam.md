# Fake High-Return Investment Platform Scam

Category: 假投資詐騙

Description:
Fraudsters recruit victims via LINE groups, social media, or romance contacts, promising extraordinary returns from stock tips, cryptocurrency, or "AI quantitative trading" platforms. They let victims withdraw small profits early to build trust, then convince them to deposit large sums before suddenly making funds inaccessible.

Common signals:
- claims of guaranteed daily or monthly returns (8%–30% per month)
- phrases like 「穩賺不賠」「高報酬」「AI量化交易」「限時優惠」「已有萬人加入」
- pressure to recruit friends or refer others to the platform
- domains using unofficial TLDs like .xyz, .top, .site with "invest", "profit", or "quant" in the name
- requests to deposit more funds to "unlock" or "release" existing profits

Example message:
> 【高報酬投資】穩賺不賠！每日收益 8–15%，已有超過 50,000 人加入！限時優惠，立即加入：https://invest-tw-profit.xyz/join

Keywords:
- 高報酬
- 穩賺不賠
- AI量化
- 量化交易
- 投資平台
- 月獲利
- 加入群組
- 限時優惠
