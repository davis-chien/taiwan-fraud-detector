# Fake Delivery / Customs Fee Scam

Category: 假包裹通知 / 關稅詐騙

Description:
Messages impersonating courier companies or customs authorities, claiming a parcel is being held and requires payment of a fee (customs duty, re-delivery fee, address correction fee) before it can be released. Victims are directed to a fake payment page where their card details are stolen, or asked to transfer money directly.

Also covers the customs clearance variant used in romance scams — where a "partner" claims their luggage or a valuable gift parcel is held at customs and needs the victim to pay release fees.

Common impersonated entities:
- 黑貓宅急便 (T-CAT), 7-11交貨便, 全家取貨, 台灣郵政, 中華郵政
- DHL, FedEx, UPS (for international parcel variants)
- 財政部關務署 (customs authority)

Key signals:
- claim that a parcel cannot be delivered due to address error or customs issue
- small fee required: 關稅, 重新配送費, 地址更新費, 保管費
- urgent deadline: 「請於24/48小時內完成付款，否則包裹將退回」
- link to a fake courier/customs portal
- romance variant: overseas partner sends valuable gift → held at customs → victim asked to pay release fee

Example messages:
> 【黑貓宅急便】您的包裹因地址不符無法配送，請點擊 https://t-cat-redelivery.site/form 更新收件資訊並繳納重新配送費NT$80。

> 財政部關務署通知：您有一件國際包裹需繳納關稅NT$2,400方可放行，請至 https://customs-tw.xyz/pay 完成繳納。

> 親愛的，我從海外寄了一份禮物給你，但被海關扣押需要繳NT$8,000的清關費，你能先幫我墊付嗎？到了台灣馬上還你。
