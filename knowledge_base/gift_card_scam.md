# Gift Card Scam — Friend / Family Impersonation

Category: 禮券詐騙 / 帳號盜用

Description:
Scammers impersonate a friend, family member, or colleague — using a new phone number, a cloned LINE account, or a hacked Facebook account — and fabricate an urgent situation requiring the victim to buy convenience store gift cards (Google Play, Steam Wallet, iTunes, LINE Points). They ask the victim to send the card codes by message, promising to repay in cash. Because the request comes from a known person's name, victims often comply without suspicion.

This is distinct from prize/lottery gift card scams. The key signal is the claimed identity of a known person combined with urgency and a gift card request.

Common personas and scripts:
- relative with broken phone: 「小美，是我！手機壞了借同事帳號傳訊，急需你幫我買Google Play卡」
- old classmate or colleague: 「老同學我是陳建宏，換號碼了，急需周轉，你能去超商買Steam卡嗎？」
- cousin or close family: 「你好，我是你表哥！用新號碼，急需你幫忙買超商禮物卡8000元」
- boss/manager: 「我是王經理，正在開會不方便打電話，幫我買iTunes卡，之後報銷」

Structural red flags:
- contact through an unfamiliar number or account claiming to be someone you know
- urgency framing: 「非常急」「今天就要」「明天一定還你」
- request to keep it quiet: 「先不要打電話給我，等我回去再說」
- convenience store gift card rather than bank transfer (harder to trace)
- asks for the card code / PIN by message after purchase

Example messages:
> 小美，是我！手機壞了借同事的帳號傳訊，我現在很急，幫我去超商買Google Play禮物卡3000元，我明天還你，先謝謝！

> 你好，我是你表哥！用新號碼找你，急需你幫忙買超商禮物卡共8000元分兩張，非常急，現金還你，先謝謝！

> 老同學！我是陳建宏，換號碼了，最近急需周轉，你能去超商買Steam錢包卡3000元幫我？真的很急，謝謝你！
