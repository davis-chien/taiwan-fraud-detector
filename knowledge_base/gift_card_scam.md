# Gift Card / Prize Scam

Category: 禮券 / 中獎詐騙

Description:
Scams that ask users to claim a prize, gift card, or bonus by clicking a link and providing personal details.

Common signals:
- mentions of 免費, 中獎, 禮券, 贈品, 大獎, 優惠
- phrases like 「恭喜您中獎」「領取獎品」「立即領取」「官方發送」
- requests for account verification,轉帳, or手續費 before receiving the reward

Example message:
> 恭喜您中獎 10,000 元現金禮券，請點擊 https://example.xyz/claim 並完成資料登錄。

Keywords:
- 中獎
- 禮券
- 免費
- 立即領取
- 大獎
- 贈品
