# LINE Group Investment Trap

Category: 假投資詐騙 / LINE群組詐騙

Description:
Victims are added to a LINE group or chat that appears to be a community of successful investors sharing tips. Group members (all scammer-controlled sock puppets) post screenshots of big profits and enthusiastically endorse a specific trading platform or stock tip. The victim is gradually recruited to invest — starting with small amounts that yield fake profits — before being encouraged to invest large sums. Withdrawal is then blocked.

Setup patterns:
- victim added to group without consent: 「您已被加入『台灣高報酬投資交流群』」
- "expert" posts real-looking profit screenshots with timestamps
- group admin sends private message offering to mentor victim personally
- platform is a fake app with a convincing UI showing fabricated P&L data

Platform types mimicked:
- forex / 外匯 trading platforms
- cryptocurrency exchanges (偽裝成 Binance、OKX 等)
- Taiwan stock options (台指期貨)
- "internal" platforms accessible only via private link

Key signals:
- 「老師帶單」「跟單操作」「保證獲利」「穩賺不賠」
- 「今天跟老師操作賺了XX%」（group member testimonial）
- 「群組限時開放，名額只剩3個」
- 「平台只接受私下轉帳入金」
- first small trade shows profit → encourages larger investment
- withdrawal requires 手續費, 稅金, or "account verification fee"

Example messages:
> 我在這個群組跟著老師操作，上週賺了8萬，真的太厲害了！老師現在還有3個名額，有興趣的私訊我。

> 【限時通知】本群投資顧問將於今晚8點分享獨家內線，名額僅剩5位，把握機會入群：https://line.me/invite/...
