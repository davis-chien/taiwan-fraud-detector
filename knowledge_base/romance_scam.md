# Romance / Relationship Scam — Trust-Building Phase

Category: 感情詐騙

Description:
Scammers initiate contact with strangers on LINE, Facebook, Instagram, or dating apps — often through a "wrong number" message or an unsolicited friend request. They spend days or weeks building emotional closeness before introducing any financial request. This early phase has no urgency keywords and no URLs; detection must rely on the pattern of a stranger rapidly building intimacy while claiming to be an overseas professional.

Common opening gambits:
- wrong number opener: 「不好意思，我找錯人了，可以繼續聊嗎？」
- mutual friend claim: 「我是你朋友XX介紹認識你的」
- social media cold approach: 「看到你的照片感覺你人很好，可以認識嗎？」
- platform: 「我在 LINE / Facebook / Instagram / 交友軟體 看到你」

Identity personas typically claimed:
- military officer stationed overseas (美軍、台灣籍軍官、UN維和部隊)
- Taiwanese professional working abroad (新加坡、日本、杜拜、美國、英國、加拿大)
- widowed or divorced doctor, engineer, or businessman
- successful investor / entrepreneur

Escalation pattern:
1. Friendly small talk, never asks for money immediately
2. Claims loneliness or recent personal hardship (divorce, death of spouse)
3. Shares personal details and photos to build credibility
4. Eventually pivots to investment opportunity or personal emergency
5. Asks victim to keep the relationship private: 「這件事先不要告訴家人」

Example messages:
> 你好，我是在美國服役的台灣籍軍官，最近太孤單了，在交友軟體找到你，感覺你人很好，可以聊聊嗎？

> 我是旅居日本的台灣商人，最近很孤獨，在臉書看到你，感覺你是個善良的人，能認識你嗎？

> 我是在加拿大行醫的台灣醫生，離婚後一個人很寂寞，在交友App看到你的資料，感覺我們很合適。
