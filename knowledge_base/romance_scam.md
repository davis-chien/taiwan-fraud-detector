# Romance / Relationship Scam

Category: 感情詐騙

Description:
Scammers build a fake romantic or friendship relationship over days or weeks — often initiating contact through a "wrong number" message or a social media friend request — before eventually asking for money, investments, or sensitive information. Victims are typically targeted on LINE, Facebook, or Instagram. The scammer often claims to be a successful Taiwanese professional working overseas.

Common signals:
- contact initiated by a stranger claiming a wrong number or mutual friend
- claims to be a Taiwanese working overseas (e.g., 「我在海外工作」, 「在新加坡/日本經商」)
- builds emotional closeness quickly over messages before asking for any favor
- eventually introduces an "investment opportunity" or claims a sudden personal emergency requiring money
- asks victim to keep the relationship secret from family

Example message:
> 您好，我是在海外工作的台灣人，看到您的資料感覺我們很有緣，能加您好友嗎？我最近有個投資機會很好，可以帶您一起賺錢。
