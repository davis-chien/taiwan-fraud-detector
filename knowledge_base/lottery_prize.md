# Lottery / Prize Scam

Category: 中獎詐騙

Description:
Victims receive messages claiming they have won a prize in a lottery, promotional draw, or brand campaign (often impersonating LINE, 7-11, or major retailers). To "claim" the prize, victims are asked to pay a processing fee, provide personal data, or click a link to a phishing site. The prize never materialises.

Common signals:
- claims the recipient is a "lucky winner" or "this month's prize winner"
- impersonates well-known brands: LINE、統一、全家、7-11、蝦皮、momo
- phrases like 「恭喜中獎」「幸運得主」「限時領取」「需繳手續費」
- a link to an unofficial domain with keywords like "prize", "lucky", "redeem", "win"
- requires payment of a small fee (typically NT$500–NT$2,000) before releasing the prize

Example message:
> 恭喜您！您的 LINE 帳號參加抽獎活動中獎，獎品為 iPhone 15 Pro，請於 48 小時內至 https://line-prize.win/claim 填寫領獎資料，需繳交手續費 500 元。

Keywords:
- 中獎
- 恭喜
- 幸運得主
- 領獎
- 手續費
- 限時
- 抽獎
- 獎品
