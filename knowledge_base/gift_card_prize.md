# Gift Card / Prize Lottery Scam

Category: 中獎詐騙

Description:
Fraudulent messages claiming the recipient has won a prize, cash reward, or gift voucher. Victims are directed to a fake website to claim the reward, where they are asked to provide personal information or pay a small "processing fee" or "tax" before receiving the prize. No prize is ever delivered.

Common signals:
- unsolicited notification of winning: 「恭喜您中獎」「您已獲得獎項」「幸運抽中」
- pressure to claim quickly: 「限今日領取」「逾期作廢」「名額有限」
- link to fake claim page
- request for personal details (ID number, bank account) or payment of 手續費 / 稅金 before prize delivery
- impersonation of known brands: 統一超商、全聯、蝦皮、momo、LINE

Example messages:
> 恭喜您中獎 NT$10,000 現金禮券，請點擊 https://example.xyz/claim 並完成資料登錄，限24小時領取。

> 【全聯福利中心】您的點數累積達到兌獎門檻，請點擊連結領取您的專屬禮券，逾期失效。

> 您被抽中為幸運用戶，可獲得iPhone 15一台，請先繳納NT$500手續費，貨到付款退回。
