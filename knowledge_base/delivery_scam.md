# Fake Delivery / Package Fee Scam

Category: 物流詐騙

Description:
Messages pretending to be from couriers or convenience stores, claiming a package delivery is pending or a fee is due to release a parcel.

Common signals:
- courier names like 黑貓, 7-11, 全家, 宅配, 郵局
- phrases like 「運費未繳」「包裹待領」「貨到付款」「手續費」「驗證身分」
- urgent pressure to click a link or pay a small fee

Example message:
> 您有一筆包裹因運費未繳而無法派送，請立刻點擊 https://example.xyz/confirm 繳納 30 元手續費。

Keywords:
- 包裹
- 運費
- 手續費
- 派送
- 取件
- 物流通知
