# Fake Bank / OTP Phishing Call or SMS

Category: 網路釣魚 / OTP盜取

Description:
Scammers send an SMS or make a call impersonating a bank's fraud alert system, claiming the victim's account has been compromised or that a suspicious transaction is pending. The victim is directed to a phishing page or asked to read back a one-time password (OTP) to "verify identity" or "block the transaction." The OTP is actually used to authorize a transfer or change account credentials.

Delivery methods:
- SMS appearing to come from the bank's official short code (SMS spoofing)
- Automated robocall followed by human agent
- LINE message impersonating bank customer service
- Push notification from a fake banking app

Key signals:
- account compromise claim: 「偵測到您的帳戶有異常交易」「您的帳號遭到未授權登入」
- OTP request: 「請提供您剛收到的驗證碼以取消交易」「為了保護您的帳戶，請告知OTP」
- account freeze threat: 「若未在30分鐘內確認，帳戶將被凍結」
- link to fake banking portal that looks like the real one
- caller asks victim to stay on the line while they "verify"

Legitimate banks NEVER:
- ask for OTP by phone or message
- ask for online banking password
- instruct customers to transfer money to a "safe account"

Example messages:
> 【玉山銀行】您的帳戶於今日14:23發生可疑交易NT$28,500，如非本人操作請立即點擊 https://esun-secure.top/block 取消。

> 台灣銀行安全通知：您的帳號已被嘗試登入3次，帳號將在1小時內凍結，請點擊 https://twbank-id.xyz/verify 完成身分驗證。

> 您好，我是國泰世華銀行客服，您的帳戶疑似遭盜用，為阻止盜刷，請告知您剛收到的6位數驗證碼。
