# Government / Official Impersonation Scam

Category: 假冒公務機關詐騙

Description:
Messages or calls impersonating Taiwan government agencies, courts, police, or prosecutors. They fabricate legal jeopardy — arrest warrants, fines, pending lawsuits, or account freezes — to pressure victims into complying with instructions, usually transferring money to a "safe account" or providing account credentials.

Two main variants:

**Variant A — Administrative threat (健保 / 稅務 / 警政)**
Claims the victim's health insurance, ID, or account has been misused or flagged for administrative violation. Directs victim to a link to "resolve" the issue.

**Variant B — Legal / criminal threat (通緝令 / 檢察署 / 法院)**
Impersonates a prosecutor, court clerk, or investigator, claiming the victim is under investigation or has an outstanding arrest warrant. Instructs victim to cooperate with an investigation by transferring funds to a "safe account" or providing bank details. Often delivered by phone but the initial contact may be a LINE message.

Variant B specific signals:
- 「地方法院」「地方檢察署」「台北地檢署」「高等法院」
- 「通緝令」「逮捕令」「拘票」
- 「涉嫌詐欺」「洗錢」「藏匿贓款」
- 「配合偵查」「保全證據」「安全帳戶」
- caller instructs victim NOT to tell family or hire a lawyer

Common signals (both variants):
- agency names: 健保署, 警政署, 165, 法院, 稅務局, 地檢署, 刑事局
- threat language: 「通緝令」「逮捕」「罰款」「法院傳票」「違規」「帳號凍結」
- urgency: must resolve today or face arrest / account freeze
- request to transfer to a 安全帳戶 or provide OTP

Example messages:
> 本機關查詢到您的身分資料涉及詐欺行為，請立即點擊 https://example.xyz/notice 查詢詳情，否則將被罰款。

> 台北地方檢察署：您涉嫌詐欺案，本署已核發通緝令，如願配合偵查可致電檢察官林○○，否則將執行逮捕。

> 您好，我是台灣高等法院書記官，您名下帳戶涉及洗錢案，需要配合本院調查，請勿告知他人。
