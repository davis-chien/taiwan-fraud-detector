# Government / Official Impersonation Scam

Category: 假冒公務機關詐騙

Description:
Messages that impersonate government agencies, police, or official services to threaten the user or demand immediate action.

Common signals:
- agency names like 健保署, 警政署, 165, 法院, 稅務, 檢察官
- threat language such as 「帳號停用」「違規」「罰款」「法院傳票」「違反規定」
- urging the user to click a link to avoid punishment or resolve a case

Example message:
> 本機關查詢到您的身分資料涉及詐欺行為，請立即點擊 https://example.xyz/notice 查詢詳情，否則將被罰款。

Keywords:
- 罰款
- 帳號停用
- 法院傳票
- 違規
- 公務機關
- 假冒警察
