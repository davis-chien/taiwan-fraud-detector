# Money Mule / Account Selling Recruitment

Category: 車手詐騙 / 帳戶販賣

Description:
Scammers recruit victims to act as money mules — either by renting/selling their bank account or by physically withdrawing and transferring cash ("車手"). Victims are often unaware that participating makes them criminally liable for money laundering. Recruitment messages are disguised as job offers, financial help, or requests from a mutual friend.

Variants:
1. **Account rental/sale:** Asks victim to sell or temporarily lend their ATM card + passbook + online banking credentials. Often framed as "account verification" or a business need.
2. **Cash withdrawal runner (車手):** Victim is asked to withdraw cash from ATMs and deliver it, earning a per-trip fee.
3. **Forwarding agent:** Victim receives transfers to their account and is asked to forward them onward, keeping a small percentage — framed as remittance or business service.
4. **Social engineering via friend:** Someone the victim knows (whose account has been compromised) asks them to accept or forward a transfer.

Key signals:
- request to sell, rent, or lend bank account / ATM card / passbook
- offer of easy money for simply "holding" or "forwarding" funds
- framing as legitimate business: 「只是幫忙轉帳，有手續費」「公司資金調度需要」
- reassurance that it is legal or that victim bears no risk
- 「您只需要提款後交給我們的人員」

Example messages:
> 您好，我是在路上撿到一個錢包，裡面有您的名片，我想要歸還，請問您什麼時候方便見面？（用於接近車手招募目標）

> 有個客戶指定要找您，說是您的老朋友，他有一筆錢想透過您處理，酬勞豐厚，請問方便聯絡嗎？

> 您好，公司需要多個帳戶進行資金調度，只需借用您的帳戶3天，每天酬勞NT$3,000，完全合法，有興趣嗎？

> 幫忙提款跑腿，每趟NT$2,000，一天可以跑3-4趟，工作輕鬆，現金當場給付。
