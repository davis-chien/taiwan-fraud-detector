# Fake Installment Cancellation Scam (解除分期付款)

Category: 解除分期付款詐騙

Description:
A two-phase scam. Phase 1 is a phone call or message claiming the victim has accidentally enrolled in a recurring charge. Phase 2 gives step-by-step ATM or online banking instructions that actually transfer money to the scammer.

Many victims receive the initial contact as a polite, low-pressure message with no ATM mention yet — the ATM instructions come later by phone. Detection at the message stage must catch Phase 1 patterns too.

**Phase 1 — Initial contact (message or call):**
- caller/sender claims to be from a bank, telecom, e-commerce platform, or consumer protection agency
- states the victim has been accidentally enrolled in a recurring installment charge or double-charged
- offers to help cancel or refund — needs victim to "cooperate" with a procedure
- no urgency yet; tone is polite and official

Phase 1 impersonated entities:
- banks: 台新銀行, 玉山銀行, 台灣銀行, 國泰世華, 中國信託
- telecoms: 中華電信, 台灣大哥大, 遠傳
- platforms: momo, 蝦皮, PChome, 全聯
- officials: 消費者保護官, 消保官, 公平交易委員會

Phase 1 specific signals:
- 「誤設分期」「重複扣款」「自動扣款」「多收費」
- 「我們需要您配合操作」「協助您取消」「引導您辦理」
- 「請問您現在方便嗎？」
- impersonates 消費者保護官: 「我是消費者保護官，偵測到商家對您重複收費」

**Phase 2 — ATM / online banking instructions:**
- step-by-step ATM operation described as "cancellation procedure"
- asks victim to transfer funds to a designated account
- claims funds will be returned immediately after cancellation is confirmed
- asks for online banking password, OTP, or credit card number for "verification"

Example messages:
> 您好，我是台新銀行信用卡中心，您的帳號誤設了每月自動扣款分期，我們需要協助您在ATM操作取消，請問您現在方便嗎？

> 您好，我是消費者保護官，偵測到商家對您重複收費，需要您配合操作解除，請準備好您的存摺和提款卡，我們將逐步引導您。

> 請您點擊以下連結取消誤設的分期付款，需要在ATM操作：https://cancel-installment.xyz/guide 請盡快處理，否則將自動扣款。
