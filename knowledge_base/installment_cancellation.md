# Fake Installment Cancellation Scam

Category: 解除分期付款詐騙

Description:
Scammers call pretending to be bank representatives or telecom customer service agents, claiming the victim has accidentally enrolled in recurring installment charges. They instruct the victim to follow steps at an ATM or online banking portal to "cancel" the subscription — steps that actually transfer money to the scammer's account.

Common signals:
- unsolicited call from someone claiming to be a bank or telecom (e.g., 中華電信, 台灣銀行, 玉山銀行)
- phrases like 「誤設分期」「解除分期付款」「ATM操作」「轉帳操作」「取消扣款」
- urgency: must be done within a few hours or a fee will be charged
- request for credit card number, CVV, OTP, or online banking password
- links directing to ATM instructions or fake banking portals

Example message:
> 請您點擊以下連結取消誤設的分期付款，需要在 ATM 操作：https://cancel-installment.xyz/guide 請盡快處理，否則將自動扣款。
