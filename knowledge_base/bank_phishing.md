# Fake Bank Login / Account Verification Scam

Category: 銀行詐騙

Description:
Fake messages claiming to be from banks or payment services, asking users to verify account details, re-login, or confirm a payment.

Common signals:
- mentions of bank names such as 玉山銀行, 土地銀行, 台灣銀行, 信用卡, ATM
- phrases like 「登入帳戶」「驗證身份」「帳號異常」「立即確認」
- URLs that mimic banking pages or use suspicious redirect domains

Example message:
> 恭喜，您有未完成的匯款交易，請立即點擊 https://example.xyz/login 進行身份驗證，否則帳號將被鎖定。

Keywords:
- 中獎
- 登入
- 驗證
- 帳號異常
- 立即確認
- 鎖定
