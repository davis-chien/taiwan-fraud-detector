# Pig-Butchering Investment Romance Scam (殺豬盤)

Category: 感情詐騙 / 假投資詐騙

Description:
殺豬盤 (pig butchering) is a long-con romance scam where the scammer spends weeks building trust and romantic attachment before introducing a "sure-win" investment platform — typically cryptocurrency or forex trading. The victim is shown fake profits and encouraged to invest more. When the victim tries to withdraw, they are told to pay fees or taxes first; once they refuse or run out of money, the scammer disappears. This is the highest-loss scam category in Taiwan.

Progression:
1. Romance / trust phase: build emotional relationship over LINE or social media (weeks)
2. Investment introduction: scammer mentions personal investment success casually
3. Platform introduction: directs victim to a fake app or website with fabricated P&L charts
4. Small wins: victim sees early fake profits, encouraged to invest more
5. Escalation: victim urged to invest life savings, loans, or borrow from family
6. Withdrawal blocked: told to pay 手續費, 稅金, or 解鎖費 before withdrawal
7. Ghost: scammer and platform disappear after victim is drained

Financial ask signals:
- 「我帶你一起投資，保證獲利」
- 「平台上個月讓我賺了XX萬」
- 「你只需要入金XX元就能開始」
- 「提款前要先繳納手續費/稅金/保證金」
- 「這個平台是我朋友內部推薦的」
- 「不要讓家人知道，這是我們的秘密」

Emergency money request variant (Romeo scam):
- stranded overseas: 「行李被海關扣留，需要繳XX元才能放行」
- medical emergency: 「我在醫院急需手術費，先借我，回去後還你」
- urgent transfer: 「銀行帳號凍結，幫我先轉到你帳號保管」

Example messages:
> 親愛的，我已經抵達杜拜，工程項目很順利！最近有個很好的投資機會，是我同事介紹的，你有興趣一起嗎？報酬很高。

> 我在海外工作有多餘資金想投資，聽說你在投資領域很有研究，我有200萬想操作，可以帶我一起嗎？

> 親愛的，我投資平台最近收益很好，上個月已賺了NT$300,000，我可以帶你一起操作，只需要NT$50,000入金就能開始。

> 親愛的，我快回台灣了！但行李被海關扣留說需要繳清關費NT$45,000才能放行，你能幫我嗎？
